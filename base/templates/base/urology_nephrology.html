Our Treatments
Effective Kidney treatment requires prompt intervention and expert care.
  • NEUROUROLOGY AND RECONSTRUCTIVE UROLOGY
  • ENDOUROLOGY AND STONE DISEASE
  • UROLOGIC ONCOLOGY
  • PROSTATE CLINIC
NEUROUROLOGY AND RECONSTRUCTIVE UROLOGY

Care for people who have neurologic conditions affecting the urinarysystem. At Medanta, urologists also offer reconstruction for people who have experienced trauma or injury of the urinary tract.

ENDOUROLOGY AND STONE DISEASE

We offer minimally invasive and non-invasive management of kidney stones and other urinary tract stones. The department is a high volume minimally invasive urology center offering management of urolithiasis, treatment of benign prostate enlargement (BPH) and Urological malignancies. It has a proven track record of high success rates with low complication rates. This is despite treating high risk profile patients- cardiac comorbid, anticoagulated patients, high risk urological malignancies etc.

UROLOGIC ONCOLOGY

Medantas urologists treat kidney cancer, bladder cancer, prostate cancer, testicular cancer, penile cancer, ureteral and renal pelvic cancers, retroperitoneal and pelvic sarcomas, Wilms' tumors, pediatric sarcomas, and other cancers.

PROSTATE CLINIC

We provide care for a wide range of conditions affecting men's health, including benign prostatic hyperplasia, voiding dysfunction, urinary incontinence, and prostate cancer screening and prevention.
