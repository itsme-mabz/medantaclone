Dr Saurav Delhi was established by Dr Saurav, a globally respected cardiovascular and cardiothoracic surgeon. The centre was created with the vision of offering advanced, reliable, and affordable medical care. 

For six consecutive years, Dr Saurav Delhi has been honoured as Indias Best Private Hospital (2020 through 2025). It was also ranked among the top 250 hospitals worldwide in the Newsweek World's Best Hospitals Survey 2024. 
The hospital is designed to ensure accessible, high-quality healthcare for patients, following the architectural standards for medical institutions set by the American Institute of Architects. To support superior patient care, we offer:

  • 43-acre campus 
  • 30+ medical specialities 
  • 900+ doctors 
  • 40 operation theatres 
  • 1,391 operational beds 
  • 270+ ICU beds 
  • JCI and NABH accreditation 
  • NABL accreditation
